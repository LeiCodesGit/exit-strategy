# Exit Strategy

A personal academic tracker built for **BS Computer Engineering** students. Monitor your course progress, track statuses, and visualize your path from enrollment to graduation.

---

## Features

- **Dashboard** — Overview of your academic progress, units completed, and active courses this term
- **Course Tracker** — View and update all your CPE courses organized by year level and term
- **Roadmap** — Visualize your critical path from current standing to graduation
- **Admin Panel** — Manage users and their course data *(admin accounts only)*
